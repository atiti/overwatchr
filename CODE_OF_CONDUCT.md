# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of behavior that help this project:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- discussing tradeoffs without personal attacks
- prioritizing clarity over point-scoring

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or discrimination
- personal insults
- bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and related community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
